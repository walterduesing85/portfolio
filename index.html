Urban geology & sustainable city development

"Geology has a key role in future urban planning to limit conflicts, reduce risks and lower the costs of subsurface challenges. By managing the subsurface based on geological knowledge and data, cities may tackle challenges for a climate-neutral future and become resilient. Geological surveys play a significant role in city development by providing the large data archives and cutting-edge expertise necessary for sustainable urban development" (The European Geosciene for Society).
